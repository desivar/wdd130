A New Hobby

While in the pandemic time, a friend bring me an old Galaxy tab 4 with a stylus. I started to draw with zero experience I choose to draw about the Scriptures to bring a light of hope to my friends and relatives. At first, my drawings were naive but all of them with a special purpose. Thanks to that gift I developed a new talent, I learned how to edit using Canva, and create messages for the Relief Society, and I even make new Instagram friends. Now I am here learning how to create web pages to put in one site my Scriptures Journaling

Positive effects of Scripture Journalign

Doing Scripture Journal will keep away the sadness Help to keep a tracking record of your faith Will enhance your creativity Will help your memory Personal revelation

Scriptures

Scripture Journalign transform the the way you study the word of God, and could begging with your favorite story from the Scriptures. It is not about how well you draw but how you perceive the word of God.

What do you need?

A Scripture specil edition with large margins. Markers, colored pencil, washi-tape, eraser, watercolors, etc. Find key words, and look at the web for the meaning. Visualize the the full concept. Choose a color palette. Then left your inner talent to make the rest.
